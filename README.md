ToolSnak's Sign PDF tool lets you add a handwritten signature to any PDF document directly in your browser. Draw your signature using your mouse or a touch screen, select the page and position, and download the signed PDF instantly — completely free, with no account required.

## ✨ Key Features

* 100% browser-based — no software installation needed
* Draw your signature with a mouse, stylus, or finger on touch screens
* Place the signature on any page of a multi-page PDF
* Choose from three signature positions: bottom-left, bottom-center, bottom-right
* Customize ink color and pen size for a natural look
* Your file never leaves your device — complete privacy
* Free to use with no watermarks or registration

## 🛠️ How to Use

1. Upload your PDF by dragging it into the drop zone or clicking Choose File
2. Draw your signature in the signature canvas using your mouse or finger
3. Select the ink color and pen size you prefer
4. Choose the page you want the signature placed on
5. Select a signature position: Bottom Right, Bottom Left, or Bottom Center
6. Click Sign PDF and download your signed document
